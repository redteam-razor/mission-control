## 💡 Pro Tips

### Email Platform:
- **ConvertKit** = better for creators, automation-friendly
- **Mailchimp** = more templates, familiar interface
- Both have free tiers (up to 500-1000 subscribers)

### Lead Magnet:
- Keep it simple: 8-10 pages max
- Use Canva templates (search "lead magnet")
- Include: Problem → 10 Solutions → Call to Action
- Export as PDF, host on Google Drive (shareable link)

### Podcast Pitching:
- Personalize every pitch (mention recent episode)
- Keep it short (200-300 words)
- Lead with value for THEIR audience
- Include 3 talking points + your bio
- Be patient: response rate is ~10-20%

### Time Management:
- **Batch content creation** (write all at once, schedule later)
- **Use templates** (save 70% of time)
- **Automate everything** (email sequences, social scheduling)
- **Protect your 10 hours/week** (calendar block: M/W/F 6-8pm, Sat 8-12pm)
