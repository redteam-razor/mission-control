# 🚀 AI Sandbox Promotor Campaign
## 90-Day Campaign to Launch & Scale Your AI Sandbox Business

**Framework:** JIcecream's Promotor Blueprint  
**Duration:** March 1 - May 31, 2026 (12 weeks)  
**Goal:** Build traffic → nurture audience → webinar conversion → first paid customers  
**Target:** R30K/month revenue to break even (12+ customers @ R2,500/mo avg)

---

## 📋 Campaign Structure

### **STEP 1: TRAFFIC** (Weeks 1-12, ongoing)
*Get eyeballs on your offer*

#### Organic Traffic (Weeks 1-8)
- **Podcasts:** Identify 10 target shows, pitch your story, secure 3-5 appearances
- **Guest Posts:** Write 3-5 high-value articles for Medium, Dev.to, TechCentral, MyBroadband
- **Networking:** Attend 2+ Cape Town tech events (Silicon Cape, StartupGrind)
- **Social Media:** Daily posting on LinkedIn + X/Twitter
  - LinkedIn: 3x/week value posts + 1x/week product tease
  - Twitter: Daily tips, threads, behind-the-scenes
- **Content:** 12 LinkedIn posts, 5 Twitter threads, 10 graphics (batched)

#### Paid Traffic (Weeks 5-12)
- **Meta Ads:** R2,000 test budget (Week 5)
  - 3 audiences: finance pros, SMB owners, tech entrepreneurs
  - 5 ad variations testing
  - Target CPA: < R200/lead
- **Retargeting Ads:** R1,500 for webinar (Week 6-8)
  - Website visitors
  - Email openers
  - Abandoned registration pages

**Key Milestones:**
- Week 4: 100+ email subscribers
- Week 8: 300+ email subscribers
- Week 12: 500+ email subscribers

---

### **STEP 2: HOLDING PATTERN** (Weeks 1-12, ongoing)
*Build trust and keep them engaged*

#### Email Newsletter (Weekly, Thursdays 10am)
- **Setup:** ConvertKit/Mailchimp (free tier) - Week 1
- **Lead Magnet:** "AI Automation Starter Kit" PDF - Week 1
- **Welcome Sequence:** 5 emails automated - Week 2
- **Weekly Newsletters:**
  1. March 21: "Why Every SMB Needs AI in 2026"
  2. March 28: "3 AI Wins from Cape Town Businesses"
  3. April 4: "Automate Without Code: Beginner Guide"
  4. April 11: "AI Sandbox Deep Dive"
  5. April 18: "Customer Spotlight + Webinar Invite"
  6. Continues weekly...

#### Content Production
- **YouTube Videos:**
  - Video #1 (Week 3): "Unboxing the AI Sandbox" (10-15 min)
  - Video #2 (Week 4): "Build Your First AI Agent" (tutorial)
  - Future: Plan 8-episode podcast "AI for Finance Pros"
- **Social Posting:**
  - Daily X/Twitter presence (tips, threads, engagement)
  - 3x/week LinkedIn (value, product updates, community)
  - Behind-the-scenes stories

**Key Principle:** Value first, promotional last. Build trust over time. No hard sell in holding pattern.

### **STEP 4: OUTCOMES** (Weeks 8-12)
*Convert, delight, or loop back*

#### Success Path:
1. **Immediate Sale (Day 0-7):**
   - Onboard within 24 hours
   - Welcome call + setup assistance
   - First automation together
   - Week 2: Success check-in
   - Week 4: Request testimonial

2. **Free Trial Signup:**
   - 7-day trial (if offering)
   - Daily check-ins
   - Convert to paid by day 5

3. **Call Booked (High-Ticket):**
   - 30-min consultation
   - Qualify + demo
   - Close on call or follow-up sequence

#### Loop Back (Non-Converters):
1. **Segment by Engagement:**
   - **Hot:** Attended webinar, didn't buy (survey why)
   - **Warm:** Registered, didn't attend (send replay + case studies)
   - **Cold:** No action (continue holding pattern)

2. **Nurture Sequence for "Hot" Leads:**
   - More case studies
   - Address objections (price, complexity, ROI)
   - Softer pitch (no pressure)
   - Invite to next webinar (June)

3. **Plan Webinar #2 (June):**
   - New hook: "AI Agents for Financial Services"
   - Target warm list again with fresh angle
   - Repeat cycle

#### Tracking & Optimization:
- **Setup (Week 1):** GA4 + Google Sheets dashboard
- **Track:** Landing visits, email signups, webinar registrations, purchases
- **Calculate:** Traffic → Leads → Webinar → Sales conversion rates
- **Review:**
  - Week 4: Traffic sources, email open rates, social engagement
  - Week 8: Full funnel analysis, calculate ROI
  - Week 12: Campaign retrospective, document learnings

**Success Criteria:**
- 5+ new customers (R12.5K-R17.5K MRR)
- 300+ engaged email subscribers
- 50+ hot leads for follow-up
- Documented playbook for next campaign

## 💡 Tips from the Promotor Blueprint

### Traffic (Step 1):
- **Organic:** Build trust, long-term. Guest appearances > guest posts.
- **Paid:** Fast results, test quickly, kill losers, scale winners.
- **Don't forget:** In-person networking. Cape Town tech scene is small = big ROI.

### Holding Pattern (Step 2):
- **Key:** Keep them interested with value, not promotions.
- **Build trust over time.** Your podcast/newsletter = your world.
- **Frequency:** Weekly email minimum. Daily social if possible.

### Selling Event (Step 3):
- **Critical:** Every campaign needs a NEW HOOK.
- **It's about the learning event, not the product.**
- **Webinar > Workshop > Email campaign > Retargeting > Direct outreach.**
- **Hook example:** "Learn to automate 5 business tasks in 60 minutes" not "Buy my sandbox."

### Outcomes (Step 4):
- **Success:** Direct sale, free trial, call booked (for high-ticket).
- **Loop back:** Didn't convert? Back to holding pattern for another shot.
- **Don't be pushy.** You have permission to promote during selling events, not everywhere.
